TaskCal is a calendar-first task manager that keeps your day in one clean, focused view. Plan, track, and complete your tasks — then see them right on your Home Screen and Lock Screen with native iOS widgets.

WHY TASKCAL

• Calendar-first — see your tasks on a real monthly calendar, with dots for busy days and a clear Today highlight.
• Fast capture — add a task in seconds, set a date, and get back to what matters.
• Inline task details — edit everything on one screen and mark tasks done with a single tap.
• Native iOS widgets — view today's tasks, a monthly calendar, and a progress ring on your Home Screen and Lock Screen, always in sync.
• Stays in sync — sign in with Google and your tasks follow you, backed up securely in the cloud.
• Light & dark mode — automatically follows your system appearance.
• Fully localized — available in English, Traditional Chinese, and Spanish.

WHAT'S NEW IN 2.0

A complete visual redesign built on the new Indigo design system — a cleaner Task Detail screen with inline editing, a smarter calendar view, refreshed Settings, and new Lock Screen and Large Home Screen widgets.
